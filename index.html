About St Tropez

St Tropez is one of the most famous towns in the French Riviera and is located in in the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur region of France. It quickly became one of the hottest spots to visit in Europe back in the 19th century and has stayed at the top spot ever since.

It's beautiful beachs, views and wine have kept tourists visiting the town for years and they aren't stopping any time soon. We want to share with you all of the amazing places to visit so we have put together a selection of places we think you need to add to your list.

Top Recommendations

In search of an endless summer? Visit Le Club 55 for great food, drink and a day at out in Pampellone.
